When the primary loan isn’t enough

When your primary lender covers most — but not all — of your capital stack, a gap lender fills the remainder. This is common on fix & flip deals, value-add projects, and acquisitions where you’ve secured a first-position loan but need additional short-term capital to close.

Gap Funding in Texas — FAQs
Do you offer gap funding in Texas?
Yes. GIREC connects real estate investors with private money lenders offering gap funding across Texas, serving Dallas–Fort Worth, Houston, Austin, and San Antonio.
What is a gap loan?
A gap loan provides the capital difference between your primary financing and the total funds needed to close a deal — bridging the “gap” so you can complete the transaction.
Is GIREC a direct gap lender?
No. GIREC is not a lender. We connect you with gap lenders in our network. All funding decisions are made by the lender.
