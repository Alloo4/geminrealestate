The Basics

What is transactional lending?

Transactional lending is short-term funding used to help real estate investors close deals where capital is needed for a very limited period. It is commonly used for double closings, wholesale transactions, assignment-related structures, and situations where a buyer needs temporary capital to complete a deal before resale or payoff.
